WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF CLASSMARKER?

Our mission is to empower educators, trainers, and business owners to train others by creating effective learning experiences through affordable exams. We believe that education is a right, not a privilege. That's why we created ClassMarker - so learning is accessible to everyone. We even have a free plan for not-for-profits and exam discounts for education.

HOW CLASSMARKER HELPFUL FOR CALCULUS INSTRUCTION?

A great resource for teaching calculus is Classmaker. With its many features and easy-to-use layout, it help teachers alike understand this challenging material. Teachers can use the platform to make curriculum-specific interactive lessons, tests, and assignments. Additionally, Classmaker gives teachers access to performance measures and real-time feedback, allowing them to pinpoint each student's areas of growth. Classmaker transforms calculus training by increasing engagement and enabling specific learning, making it more efficient and accessible for all.

HOW TO CREATE TEST IN CLASSMARKER?

STEP 1. Go to the Tests section.

STEP 2. Select 'New Test'.

STEP 3. Enter a Test name and select 'Start Adding Questions'.

STEP 4. Congratulations! You have created your first Test. You can now start adding Questions.
