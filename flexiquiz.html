FLEXIQUIZ

FLEXIQUIZ

FlexiQuiz is an online test maker that allows teachers to create and share a wide variety of quizzes, assessments, and exams. It offers an intuitive and easy to use editor that can help you configure your quiz the way you want. FlexiQuiz is free to use but the free version is limited in features, the pro version (i.e., Essentials, Premium, and Enterprise plans) offer enhanced features.

WHAT ARE THE FLEXIQUIZ FEATURES?

Depending on which subscription plan you use, FlexiQuiz offers a wide range of features including:

  1. Question types:

    FlexiQuiz offers numerous question types such as fill-in-the-blanks, file upload, multiple-hoice, short answer, single choice, picture choice, essay, matching text, and more. You can choose whether you want to make a question optional or required and whether you want to receive question feedback in real time or at the end. Flexiquiz also allows you to upload supporting documents to your quizzes such as PDFs, Excel files, and Word documents.

  2. Quiz Layout

    FlexiQuiz helps you create visually engaging quizzes and exams. You can select the background of your choice, add images, select from numerous page layouts and types, and more. You can also enrich your quiz with multimedia materials such as videos, GIFs, pictures, and audio.

  3. Customization features

    FlexiQuiz quiz maker allows you to edit and customize your quiz the way you want. For instance, you can choose whether you want to time limit your quiz by page or by the whole quiz, whether to turn on/off question randomization, maximize quiz attempts, bookmark questions, progress bars, save and continue later, custom results page, email notifications, and more.

  4. Scoring and grading

    As far as scoring and grading are concerned, FlexiQuiz offers various options including: the ability to award positive or negative points for individual answers or the entire question, the ability to ‘set grade-based redirects to another test or URL, show scores by category, share a custom results page with relevant scores and data, share results during or after the quiz is submitted, show feedback based on a pass or fail score’, and more.

  5. Custom certificates

    FlexiQuiz enables you to create custom certificates to share with students upon their completion of the test. You can choose from several pre-designed templates or create your own from scratch. You can add students names, scores, certificate number, and exam completion date. Students can download their certificates from the results page or email notification.

  6. Assign collaborators

    FlexiQuiz enables you to add collaborators to your quizzes. You can set different permissions for your collaborators. For instance, you can choose whether you want them to be able to create and edit quizzes and courses, publish and share quizzes, manage results, and analyze reports.

  7. FlexiQuiz reports

    FlexiQuiz saves all responses in real-time. Using the Analyze feature, test creators can access various types of reports. They can also manage results at different levels (e.g., a test, quiz taker, or group level) and view responses submitted by individuals or groups. You also have the option to download test reports in PDF or Excel file format.

  8. Quiz types

    FlexiQuiz allows you to create a wide range of quiz types including: assessments, aptitude tests, matching tests, trivia, true or false, yes or no quizzes, spelling tests, mathematical tests, certification exams, lead generation, live quiz events, practice tests, vocabulary tests, knowledge tests, and more.

HOW DOES FLEXIQUIZ WORK?

1. Create

Quickly create great looking tests using multiple question types and formatting options.

2. Publish

Tests can either be published privately to a select group or open them up to everyone with a single link and registration page.

3. Analyze

FlexiQuiz instantly marks and grades your tests. Powerful reports then allow you to perform in-depth analysis across all responses.

HOW TO CREATE QUIZ IN FLEXIQUIZ?

Follow these step to get started creating a quiz in FlexiQuiz

STEP 1. Login to your FlexiQuiz account and click on Create Quiz button.

STEP 2. Choose one of the two quiz types provided: Classic (participants access it through a generated URL or through their accounts) and Live (create and host a quiz in real-time)

STEP 3. Type a name for your quiz and click on Create quiz

STEP 4. Use the editor to populate your quiz.

STEP 5. Click on the Configure tab to customize your quiz.

STEP 6. Click on the Preview icon to view how your quiz looks and works

STEP 7. Click on Publish Quiz to choose how you want to share your quiz.
