HOW DOES THINKSTER MATH WORKS?

Thinkster coaches don’t just observe how our students work out problems. They understand and visualize their thinking using our Active Replay Technology (ART), identify where students make mistakes, and provide guidance so students can solve new problems that improve their understanding of math.

HOW TO BEGIN THE SKILLS ASSESSMENT IN THINKSTER MATH?

Completing the assessment is the first step to a personalized learning plan!

  • Thinkster works on any tablet, laptop, or computer with Google Chrome or Safari. Kindles and phones are not supported.

  • If on a computer, laptop, or Android tablet, please log in to our Student Web App by using either Google Chrome or Safari.

  • If you have an iPad, be sure to download the app "Thinkster Math Tutoring" directly onto the device! We recommend that the iPad has iOS10 or later.

  • Once logged into the web or iPad app, you will click on your child's name and be taken to their learning dashboard. You will see the Skills Assessment for them here!
