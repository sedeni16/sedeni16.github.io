Slide Share

SlideShare is an online slide hosting service, owned by LinkedIn, where users can upload and publicly or privately share tutorials and presentations in formats such as PowerPoint, PDF, Keynote, OpenDocument etc.

How to Use the Slide Share?

Create a Slide Deck

Use presentation software such as Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, or another tool to create your calculus presentation.

Save as PDF (Optional)

If you plan to upload your presentation to SlideShare, you may want to save it as a PDF file. Many presentation tools offer the option to export or save presentations in PDF format.

Sign In to SlideShare

Visit the SlideShare website (www.slideshare.net) and sign in to your account. If you don't have an account, you'll need to create one.

Upload Your Presentation

Once signed in, click on the "Upload" button on the top right corner of the SlideShare homepage. Follow the prompts to upload your calculus presentation.

Add Title and Description

Fill in the title and description for your presentation. Make sure the title clearly reflects the content of your calculus presentation.

Choose Visibility Settings

Decide on the visibility settings for your presentation. You can choose to make it public, private, or visible to only specific people.

Add Tags (Optional)

Add relevant tags to help users find your presentation when searching on SlideShare.

Set Language and Category

Set the language of your presentation and choose the appropriate category (e.g., Education, Science) to help categorize your content.

Publish Your Presentation

Click the "Publish" button to make your calculus presentation live on SlideShare. You will receive a link to your presentation, and it will be accessible to SlideShare users.
