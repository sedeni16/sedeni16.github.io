Microsoft Sway

Sway is a new app from Microsoft Office that makes it easy to create and share interactive reports, personal stories, presentations, and more. Start by adding your own text and pictures, search for and import relevant content from other sources, and then watch Sway do the rest.

How to Use the Microsoft Sway?

STEP 1: Access Microsoft Sway

Open your web browser and go to the Microsoft Sway website (sway.office.com). Sign in with your Microsoft account or create a new account if you don't have one.

STEP 1: Start a New Sway

Once you're signed in, click on the "+ Create" button to start a new Sway.

STEP 2: Choose a Design:

Select a design theme for your Sway. Microsoft Sway offers a variety of design options to give your presentation a cohesive and visually appealing look.

STEP 3: Include Text and Headers

Use text cards to input explanations, bullet points, and headers. Sway automatically formats text for a clean and modern look.

STEP 4: Incorporate Equations and Formulas

For mathematical equations, you can use the "Text" card and insert equations using LaTeX syntax or upload images of equations.

STEP 5: Insert Visuals

Enhance your presentation with visuals. Use the "Image" card to insert images, graphs, or charts related to calculus. You can also embed content from other sources.

STEP 6: Embed Videos or Media

Use the "Embed" card to insert videos or media relevant to your calculus presentation. This can include instructional videos, animations, or interactive content.

STEP 7: Customize Design

Customize the design of your Sway by adjusting the styling options. You can change the theme, fonts, and color schemes to match your preferences or the subject matter.

STEP 8: Add Interactive Features

Explore interactive features like the "Stack" card to create a sequence of related content. You can also use the "Comparison" card for side-by-side comparisons of calculus concepts.

STEP 9: Preview Your Sway

Before finalizing, use the "Preview" button to see how your Sway will look to your audience.

STEP 10: Reorder Cards (if needed)

Rearrange cards to ensure a logical flow. Click and drag cards to reorder them.

STEP 11: Autoplay Settings (Optional)

Click on the "Share" button to generate a link to your Sway. You can share the link directly with your audience or collaborators.

STEP 12: Share Your Sway

Rearrange cards to ensure a logical flow. Click and drag cards to reorder them.

STEP 13: Collaborate (Optional)

If you're working with others, use the "Share" feature to collaborate on the same Sway in real-time.

STEP 14: Present Your Sway

Use the "Play" button to present your Sway. Sway offers a responsive and dynamic viewing experience.

STEP 15: Save Your Sway

Your Sway is automatically saved as you work. However, you can use the "Save" button to make sure your latest changes are saved.

STEP 16: Export as PDF (Optional)

If you need a static version of your Sway, you can export it as a PDF. Access this option in the "Export" menu.
