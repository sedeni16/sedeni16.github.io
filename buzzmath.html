BUZZMATH

It is a platform that facilitates the teaching and learning of mathematics through interactive exercises and activities. It is designed for students from 6 to 16 years old.

The teacher can use BuzzMath as a support tool for math classes. Students solve thousands of math problems in a fun and interactive way to help them improve their math skills. The platform allows the teacher to follow the individual progress of their students through detailed reports, this allows them to detect concepts and topics where students have greater difficulty and thus be able to support them in their learning.

WHAT ARE THE FUNCTIONALITIES OF BUZZMATH?

  • Provides interactive math problems according to the age of the student

  • Provides reports to the teacher

  • Show mistakes to students for improvement

  • Allows the student to choose different categories

  • It allows you to visualize your progress in real time.

HOW TO GET START WITH BUZZMATH?

Station 1: Login

Before you start, note that parents and children log in the same way. That means you’re using the same account, with the same email and the same password as your children.

STEP 1. Go to the Buzzmath website

Go to the Buzzmath website, then click or tap the “Login” tab at the top right of the page.

STEP 2. Click or tap on the “Family Account” tab.

STEP 3. Enter your full name or email and the password used when you created the family account.

STEP 4. From the user homepage, select the account you wish to use, represented by a character.

The parent account is always situated to the far left.

Station 2: Account personalization

A Buzzmath account can be very different from one user to the next. Follow The first steps in Buzzmath to personalize your account

STEP 1. Log in to the platform as a parent or child to personalize your account.

STEP 2. Click or tap your avatar, also called the “My Account” tab, at the bottom left in the sidebar. From there, you can modify your account.

STEP 3. Change the background by clicking or tapping the “Change the background” tab, at the top right of the screen. You’ll see your choices!

STEP 4. You can also change the character for your account by clicking or tapping the pencil that appears beside it.
