Creating a quiz is super easy with Fyrebox.The quiz maker contains a lot of other great features, which enable you to set a time limit, customize the look and feel of your quiz, and even use your own custom background image. When you want to create quizzes to build strong rapport with your blog readers, and give potential clients a taste of your expertise, you should consider using Fyrebox

HOW TO CREATE IMAGE QUIZ IN FRYEBOX?

STEP 1. Create an account

Choose an plan. You can start with a FREE account and upgrade to the Standard Plan when it feels right

STEP 2. Create your Quiz and upload your Images

Select the Yes/No Quiz, MCQ or MCQ1+, upload your content (instructions, answers, images). You can easily crop your images with our interactive crop tool.

STEP 3. Embed your Quiz on your website

Use the Wordpress or Joomla plugin and enter a shortcode to display your quiz on any page or post. If you use any other technology, use the iframe section (we'll show you how)
