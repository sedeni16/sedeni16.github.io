Integral Calculator

A calculator allows you to check your solutions to calculus exercises. It helps you practice by showing you the full working (step by step integration). All common integration techniques and even special functions are supported.

How to Use the Integral Calculator App?

STEP 1: Download and install

Search for an integral calculator app in your preferred app store, such as the Google Play Store for Android or the App Store for iOS. Download and install the app on your device.

STEP 2: Launch the app

Launch the integral calculator app on your device.

STEP 3: Input the Function

Using the provided input field or calculator interface, enter the function that you want to integrate. Type the function using proper syntax and follow any guidelines provided by the app.

STEP 4: Specify Integration Bounds

Indicate the integration bounds if you are looking to compute a definite integral. Enter the lower and upper limits of integration either through designated input fields or by specifying them within the app's interface.

STEP 5: Choose Integration Method

Depending on the app's capabilities, you may have options to select different integration methods, such as the midpoint rule, Simpson's rule, or more advanced numerical techniques. Choose the appropriate integration method based on your requirements or preferences.

STEP 6: Calculate the Integral

Once you have inputted the function and specified the integration bounds and method, proceed to calculate the integral. Tap the "Calculate" or equivalent button in the app's interface.

STEP 7: View and Analyze Results

The integral calculator app should display the result of the integral calculation on the screen. The output may be shown as a simplified expression, a decimal approximation, or in other forms based on the app's features.

STEP 8: Analyze the Result

Examine the obtained integral and interpret its meaning in the context of the original function. Consider factors such as area under the curve, accumulated quantities, or any other relevant interpretations.

STEP 9: Additional Features

Some integral calculator apps offer additional features like step-by-step solutions, graphs of the integrated function, or the ability to compute multiple integrals. Explore the app to see if it offers any supplementary functions that can assist in your calculus studies.

STEP 10: Customize Settings and Preferences

Depending on the app, you may have options to customize settings such as decimal precision, output formatting, or theme preferences. Utilize these settings to match your preferences or specific needs.
