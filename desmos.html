Desmos

An online, interactive graphing calculator and mathematical software that enables users to graph, visualize, and manipulate mathematical functions and equations in both 2D and 3D. In the context of calculus, Desmos can help students and educators explore and understand calculus concepts through interactive and dynamic graphing and analysis.

How to Use the Desmos App?

STEP 1: Access Desmos

Open a web browser and go to the Desmos website (https://www.desmos.com/). You can use Desmos for free without the need to download or install any software.

STEP 2: Familiarize Yourself with the Interface:

When you first open Desmos, you'll see a blank graphing canvas. On the left, you have a keypad for entering mathematical expressions. On the right, there's a sidebar with options for customization and analysis. Enter Calculus Functions: Use the keypad to enter the calculus function you want to explore.
