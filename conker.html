CONKER AI

CONKER AI

an artificially intelligent quiz creation platform that is based online and can be accessed by anyone using a web browser window. All this comes from the creators who brought us voice note taking tool Mote, so the end result is a clearly well-conceived design that works. Conker is an artificial intelligence platform that is able to quickly create a quiz, which can be very helpful for educators who want to save time.

HOW DOES CONKER WORK?

Conker can be accessed online, where you are met with an input dialogue box that helpfully lays out how you can structure your request. Complete the parts you need, including the quiz output type and the subject matter, then the AI will get to work generating the quiz result for you.

You are then able to preview what is created as well as check answers. Finally, you can export the quiz ready to share with students. This can be done in a number of ways, including sharing a code that students, even without an account, can access right away using a web browser on nearly any device. Other ways to share are available but more on that in the next section.

This is built to be used by educators but also by students, parents, and other stakeholders. That said, it's not COPPA compliant, so be wary if you do decide to let students use this. You will need a Google account to log in, however, and if you have that then the process of getting up and running takes less than a minute.

HOW CONKER AI HELFUL FOR CALCULUS?

There is no denying Conker AI's usefulness for math. It is capable of solving challenging mathematical problems fast due to its advanced algorithms and machine learning capabilities.

Conker AI also gives students detailed explanations so they may comprehend the fundamental ideas supporting each calculation. It is an essential instrument for calculus students because of its capacity to manage big datasets and carry out complex calculations.

Conker AI, in summary, transforms the way we teach calculus by making it easier to understand and more approachable for students of all skill levels.

How to Use the CONKER AI?

STEP 1: LOG IN TO CONKER

1. Visit the Conker website by navigating to www.conkeral.com.

2. Log in to your Conker Al account. If you don't have one, you can quickly create an account by following the registration process.

STEP 2: SELECT A TOPIC

Once you're logged in, you'll land on the Conker Al dashboard.

2. Click on the "Create New Quiz" button.

3. Select the topic or subject for your quiz. You can choose from a wide range of categories to suit your needs.

STEP 3:CHOOSE QUESTION TYPES

1. After selecting a topic, you'll be prompted to choose the types of questions you want to include in your quiz. Conker Al offers a variety of question formats, ensuring your quiz is engaging and informative.

2. You can mix and match question types to create a well-rounded quiz experience.

STEP 4: GENERATE YOUR QUIZ

1. With your topic and question types chosen, click the "Generate Quiz" button.

2. Conker Al's powerful algorithms will swiftly create a unique quiz tailored to your specifications.

3. Review the quiz to ensure it meets your expectations.

STEP 5: EXPORT TO GOOGLE DRIVE

1. Once you're satisfied with the quiz, click the "Export to Google Drive" button.

2. Conker Al will seamlessly transfer your quiz to your Google Drive account.

3. From there, you can easily share the quiz with your students, colleagues, or audience.
