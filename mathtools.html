HiPER Scientific Calculator is a powerful calculator app available for Android devices that offers advanced mathematical functions, including those used in calculus. It is designed to provide a comprehensive set of tools for both simple and complex calculations.

In terms of calculus, the HiPER Scientific Calculator app provides several functions and features that are useful for performing calculus operations.

How to Use the Math Tools App?

STEP 1: Download and install

Visit the Google Play Store on your Android device and search for "HiPER Scientific Calculator." Download and install the app on your device.

STEP 2: Familiarize Yourself with the Interface

Open the app and take a moment to explore the calculator's interface. HiPER Scientific Calculator offers a feature-rich layout, so it's important to get acquainted with the various buttons and functions available.

STEP 3: Basic arithmetic operations

HiPER Scientific Calculator can perform basic arithmetic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Use the corresponding buttons on the calculator interface to input and calculate these operations.

STEP 4: Trigonometric functions:

The app supports trigonometric functions such as sine, cosine, tangent, and their inverses. Look for buttons labeled "sin," "cos," "tan," "asin," "acos," and "atan" to perform calculations involving trigonometry.

STEP 5: Logarithmic and exponential functions

HiPER Scientific Calculator allows you to calculate logarithms and exponentials. Locate buttons labeled "log," "ln," "10^x," and "e^x" for these operations.

STEP 6: Differentiation:

To calculate derivatives of functions, input the desired function and specify the variable. Look for buttons like "d/dx" or "diff" to perform differentiation.
