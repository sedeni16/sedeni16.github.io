HOW TO USED ALMANACK AI?

Firstly, you’ll need to go to Almanack.ai and log in with your free account. Once you’re in, you’ll see a range of features on the left panel, and we’re going to start with lesson planning.

Plan a Course

Click on the “Plan a Course” button.

You can choose between using a standardized curriculum or adding your own.

Select the curriculum that suits your needs. For example, if you’re a chemistry teacher, find the relevant course.

Add Units

Once you're logged in, you'll land on the Conker Al dashboard.

1. Click on the course you’ve selected

2. You can either manually add units or use AI to suggest units.

3. AI-generated units can be moved around or deleted as needed.

Add Lessons

Within each unit, you can add lessons. Here’s how:

1. Click on the unit name in teal.

2. Review the lessons and modify the learning outcomes as necessary.

3. Almanack even lets you create your own learning resources for these lessons.

Video Recommendations

If you’re looking for educational videos related to your lesson, Almanack makes it easy:

1. Click on “Video Recommendations.”

2. Browse a selection of high-quality videos to include in your curriculum.

Create a Worksheet

Creating worksheets has never been easier. You can customize them based on what you’re covering in class and your student’s interests.

Here’s how:

1. Select the type of questions you want (e.g., multiple-choice, short answers, labelling).

2. Customize the worksheet by adding specific questions or instructions.

3. Click “Create” to generate your custom worksheet.

Export Worksheets

You can export the worksheet in various formats, including PDF, Google Docs, or Microsoft Word documents.

Schedule your lessons

Almanack allows you to schedule your lessons conveniently.

Here’s how:

1. Toggle the scheduling option at the top.

2. Input your start date and meeting times.

3. Almanack will automatically schedule your lessons according to your preferences.

Reference Past Resources

Accessing your past resources is a breeze:

1. Click on the “Resource” button on the left panel.

2. Find all your resources neatly organized in one place.
