Venngage

Venngage is an online tool for creating infographics, reports, and data visualizations. First, users choose a template or color scheme, then they can add text, change fonts, and add widgets, charts, and icons (among many other features)

How to Use the Venngage?

Sign Up or Log In

Visit the Venngage website and sign up for an account. If you already have an account, log in.

Start a New Project

After logging in, click on the "Create" button or "New" to start a new project. Choose the presentation template category.

Select a Template

Venngage provides various templates. Choose a template that aligns with the visual style you want for your calculus presentation.

Add Slides

Venngage works with a scroll-down format, but you can think of each section as a slide. Add new sections to your project to represent different topics or concepts within calculus.

Add Title and Content

In each section, add a title and content. Use the available text boxes to input explanations, bullet points, and headers.

Incorporate Equations and Formulas

For mathematical equations, use text boxes to write equations using LaTeX syntax. Alternatively, you can create equations in another tool and upload them as images.

Insert Visuals

Enhance your presentation with visuals. Use icons, images, and charts available in the Venngage library. Upload your own images if needed.

Customize Design

Customize the design of your presentation by adjusting colors, fonts, and backgrounds. Venngage provides a range of customization options.

Use Infographic Elements

Venngage is known for its infographic elements. Incorporate these elements to visualize data or concepts in your calculus presentation.

Add Interactive Elements (if needed)

Venngage may offer some interactive elements. Explore these features if you want to add interactive elements to your presentation.

Preview Your Presentation

Before finalizing, preview your entire project to ensure that the flow is logical and that the design looks cohesive.

Share Your Venngage Project

Share your Venngage project by using the sharing options available in the platform. You can generate a link or embed the project.

Download Your Project (Optional)

If you need to present offline or use a different platform, you can download your Venngage project as an image or PDF.

Present Your Venngage Project

While Venngage doesn't have a built-in presentation mode, you can guide your audience through your project by scrolling down. Share your screen during an online presentation or use it as a visual aid during a live presentation.
