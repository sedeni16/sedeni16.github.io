Zoho Shows

Zoho Show lets you create, collaborate, present, broadcast, and publish presentations, the smarter way. Make your ideas resonate. Zoho Show lets you access your presentations even when you are not connected to the internet.

How to Use the Zoho Shows?

Open the app and click the plus (+) sign to create

Choose a better template and click check.

Type a Title or topic.

Add another slide and choose a template for the next slide.

In another slide, put the topic or exercises you choose.

Add again another slide to put another topic or answer.

Do it repeatedly until the last part of your exercises or topic.

After putting those topics, you can now animate or add transitions every slides.

And Lastly, You can now save and present your Powerpoint presentation inside the class by pressing the play button.
